Welcome from the President and Vice-Chancellor

Class of 2024, congratulations!

Today is a very special occasion as we celebrate your graduation. We are very proud of your achievements and we wish you every success in your plans for the future.

However, this is not goodbye. Now that you have graduated, you join a worldwide community of more than 300,000 alumni who continue to be an important part of our University. We hope that you will keep in touch with us, and with each other, in the years to come.

I would also like to offer a very warm welcome to your family and friends, who have supported you during your time with us. I hope you all enjoy this momentous day.

Professor Koen Lamberts
